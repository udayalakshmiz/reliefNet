About ReliefNet

Our Mission

ReliefNet is a comprehensive disaster relief coordination platform designed to bridge the gap between victims, volunteers, and relief organizations during emergencies. Our platform enables rapid response, efficient resource allocation, and seamless communication during critical situations.

How It Works

1

Report Emergency

Victims can quickly report their location and the type of help needed through our platform.

2

Connect with Volunteers

Nearby volunteers and NGOs receive notifications and can respond immediately.

3

Coordinate Relief

Our platform facilitates coordination between all parties to ensure efficient relief delivery.

Key Features

  • 24/7 emergency support
